Under the Hood

Technology Deep Dive

ZYNTRA's platform is built on a modern stack of AI and geospatial components — from satellite data ingestion pipelines to machine learning inference engines — designed for complex industrial and environmental data environments.

01 / ACQUISITION
🛰️

Satellite Data Engine

ZYNTRA's ingestion layer continuously pulls multi-spectral and SAR satellite imagery from commercial and open constellations, handling terabyte-scale volumes in near-real-time.

  • Multi-spectral & SAR imagery fusion
  • Hyperspectral band processing
  • Cloud-native geospatial pipelines
  • Automated orthorectification & tiling
02 / INTELLIGENCE
🧠

AI & Deep Learning Core

Custom-trained convolutional and transformer-based neural networks identify geological structures, spectral anomalies, and surface mineralogy signatures with high accuracy.

  • Custom CNN + ViT hybrid architecture
  • Transfer learning on geological datasets
  • Anomaly detection & spectral matching
  • Confidence-scored output layers
03 / PREDICTION
🎯

Mineral Zone Prediction

Probabilistic mineral zone maps are generated by fusing AI output with geological vector data, elevation models, and historical survey records into a unified intelligence layer.

  • Multi-layer geospatial fusion
  • Bayesian probability scoring
  • DEM + fault-line correlation
  • Confidence-heat map generation
04 / DELIVERY
📊

Decision Intelligence Layer

Actionable reports, interactive GIS dashboards, and API outputs are delivered to decision-makers, geologists, and enterprise systems in structured, field-ready formats.

  • GIS-ready output (GeoJSON, SHP, KML)
  • RESTful API for enterprise integration
  • Interactive web dashboard
  • PDF / executive report generation

Next-Generation Capability

3D Geological Digital Twin

ZYNTRA is developing the Kingdom's first AI-powered 3D geological digital twin — a real-time, continuously updated virtual model of Saudi Arabia's subsurface. By fusing satellite data, seismic records, drilling reports, and AI inference, QASSAS will generate living 3D maps of mineral zones that update as new data arrives.

Digital Twin Capabilities

🌐

Real-Time 3D Subsurface Model

Continuously updated 3D geological model of the Arabian Shield — integrating satellite imagery, DEM data, seismic surveys, and historical drill logs into a single unified digital twin.

🧩

Multi-Layer Data Fusion

Overlays surface geology, fault lines, mineral probability layers, historical exploration boundaries, and live satellite feeds — all navigable in an interactive 3D environment.

🎯

AI-Driven Drill Target Selection

The digital twin enables QASSAS to rank drill targets with precision confidence scores — eliminating blind fieldwork and directing exploration investment to the highest-probability zones.

🔄

Living Intelligence — Always Current

Unlike static geological maps, the ZYNTRA Digital Twin updates automatically as new satellite passes, exploration data, and AI analysis become available — delivering intelligence that is always current.

Digital Twin Development

Currently in active R&D — Phase 1 prototype targeting Q4 2026 with Arabian Shield sector coverage.
